Our Mission

OK Autonomous is Tulsa's dedicated commercial representative for the autonomous systems era. We serve as the shared sales force for Oklahoma's small and midsized manufacturers — sourcing qualified opportunities, facilitating buyer introductions, and translating manufacturing strengths into revenue.

We're not consultants. We're operators — rooted in the Tulsa innovation ecosystem, with 40+ years of aerospace sales, CNC and manufacturing leadership, and proven startup scaling behind us.

Our vision: Oklahoma as the drone capital of the world. The groundwork is being laid right now. When the flood comes, our manufacturers will be ready.

"A patient investment in sales and marketing will first produce a few drips, then a light trickle — followed by a flood. If the groundwork isn't in place, the flood will occur and NE Oklahoma manufacturers will miss the rewards."
